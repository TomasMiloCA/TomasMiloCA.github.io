XBRL Insights

XBRL Insights is not just another financial data database; it uses Machine Learning to cut down data procesing time by half. Leveraging Extensible Business Reporting Language (XBRL), the platform converts complex financial data into easy-to-read Excel documents. With over 100 million rows of data and information from more than 1000 firms, it serves as an invaluable resource for researchers, analysts, and decision-makers.
