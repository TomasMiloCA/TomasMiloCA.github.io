Welcome to Tomas Milo's Research Hub

I am Tomas Milo, a PhD candidate in Accounting at McGill University. My research focuses on leveraging Machine Learning techniques to structure and analyze financial data. With a background in Python programming, I develop tools that transform complex data into actionable insights.

Before embarking on my academic journey, I gained industry experience as a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) in Toronto.

XBRL Insights

XBRL Insights is not just another financial data database; it's a powerful tool designed to simplify financial analysis. Leveraging Extensible Business Reporting Language (XBRL), the platform converts complex financial data into easy-to-read Excel documents. With over 100 million rows of data and information from more than 1000 firms, it serves as an invaluable resource for researchers, analysts, and decision-makers.
